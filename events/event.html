CODESTORM

CodeStorm is a platform to put your coding skills to the test! Do you have what it takes to belittle the coders around you? If yes, then look no further!
CodeStorm is a team event which consists of 2 rounds :-

Round 1: In this round, the participating teams will have to complete a written test based on various programming concepts. The teams qualifying to the 2nd round will be shortlisted based on the scores obtained by the respective teams in this written round.
Round 2: In this round, the shortlisted teams would be provided a series of competitive programming challenges and the respective teams will be scored based on the speed and accuracy of their solution.

Note: There is no constraint on the programming language to be used in this event. Any programming language is well accepted.

SUMMIT

IEEE UVCE's flagship event Summit has been one of the most interesting events till date and it's now entering the arena with a better version! Summit is a student parliament where participants present their ideas and views on a certain topic, to the judges and audience. There will be a panel discussion amongst the renowned panellists and the moderator prior to the rounds of event. There are two rounds and they are as described below.
ROUND 1: The participants would be given a problem statement and are expected to come up with interesting solutions and present their ideas along with a ppt. The best ones would make it to the final round.
ROUND 2: This would be the final round of the event which is of similar format but with a more challenging problem statement. Judgement would be based on various criteria which would be disclosed later. The students would also be given a problem statement on spot and they are required to present their unique solution for the same without a ppt.

Registrations are closed!

LAZER TAG

Lazer Tag is a fun event where you get to experience a "First Person Shooter" in real life . Each participant will be given a laser gun and a body armour. The participants will have to form a team and compete against the other team. The participants can use the help of obstacles to evade from their opponents and shoot the opponents to gain points for their respective team. At the end of the allotted time, the team with the most number of "kills" wins.
